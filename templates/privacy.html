Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our WhatsApp Message Sender application ("the Service").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you provide when using our Service, including:

  • Contact information for your guests (names and phone numbers)
  • WhatsApp Business account information
  • Message content, including text, images, and links that you create and send
  • Responses received from message recipients

1.2 WhatsApp Platform Data

When you connect your WhatsApp Business account to our Service, we may access and process:

  • WhatsApp Business profile information
  • Contact lists from your WhatsApp Business account (if you choose to share them)
  • Message history (if you choose to share it during account connection)
  • Message status information (delivered, read, etc.)
  • Media content sent or received through the WhatsApp Business API

1.3 Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information when you use our Service:

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and settings
  • Usage data and interaction with our Service
  • Login information and session data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Service
  • Process and complete transactions
  • Send WhatsApp messages on your behalf to your selected recipients
  • Connect your WhatsApp Business account with our Service
  • Synchronize contacts and message history between WhatsApp and our Service
  • Track message delivery and response status
  • Provide technical support and respond to inquiries
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our Service
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Information

We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Meta Platform: We share information with Meta (Facebook) as necessary to integrate with the WhatsApp Business API and provide our Service.
  • Service Providers: We may use third-party service providers to help us operate our business and deliver services. These providers have access to your information only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated to protect your information.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Protection of Rights: We may disclose information to protect the safety, rights, or property of our users, ourselves, or others.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. WhatsApp Platform Terms Compliance

Our use of WhatsApp Platform Data is governed by Meta's Platform Terms and Developer Policies. Specifically:

  • We only access and use WhatsApp Platform Data for the purpose of enabling our Service's functionality.
  • We do not use WhatsApp Platform Data for advertising purposes.
  • We do not transfer or share WhatsApp Platform Data with third parties except as necessary to provide our Service.
  • We implement and maintain appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect WhatsApp Platform Data.
  • We delete WhatsApp Platform Data when it is no longer needed for the purpose for which it was collected.
  • Users can request deletion of their WhatsApp Platform Data by contacting us.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our Service. We may retain certain information to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

WhatsApp Platform Data, including message history and contact information, is retained only as long as necessary to provide our Service. You can request deletion of your WhatsApp Platform Data at any time.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Accessing, correcting, or deleting your information
  • Restricting or objecting to our processing of your information
  • Receiving a copy of your information in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Withdrawing consent at any time where we rely on consent to process your information

To exercise these rights or make a request regarding your information, please contact us using the details provided below.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete such information.

9. International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than the country in which you reside. These countries may have data protection laws that are different from the laws of your country.
